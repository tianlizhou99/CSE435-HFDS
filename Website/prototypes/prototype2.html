Instructions

Controls
W = Accelerate
S = Decelerate
A = Left Turn
D = Right Turn
B = Brake
H = Toggle HFDS

Toggles
F = Trigger HFDS fault
T = Toggle light rain
Y = Toggle heavy rain
U = Toggle other driver
P = Toggle driver attentiveness
R = Restart Prototype
